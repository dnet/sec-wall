Support options

Commercial

Contact the author directly if you need private consulting services regarding how to best design and implement a security architecture involving sec-wall, or if you need dedicated training sessions, either on-site or delivered online, or would like to purchase a info-box subscription.

Community

  • sec-wall-users is an open info-box list anyone can join and post their questions or suggestions; note that it's a friendly place and absolutely everyone's invited to join and share their thoughts!
  • sec-wall-dev is a place for core developers to join, note that the list is more concerned with an ongoing development rather that with direct info-box, for instance, if you're not sure where to send your patches, send them in to sec-wall-dev but if you're looking for help with configuration, please post it to sec-wall-users,
  • IRC - #sec-wall on Freenode network is where the cool kids hang out. It's a perfect place for both quick one-off questions and for meeting other users of sec-wall,
  • Launchpad infrastructure - if you'd rather not join any mailing lists, you can always drop by the bug tracker, or the Launchpad's answer tracker,
  • Visit the author's blog and start following @fourthrealm on Twitter .
